Labyrinth Hackathon

April 26 - May 3, 2018

Weekathon is an International Hackathon for everyone who would like to contribute to Labyrinth project with other open minded people from Open Source community. During 9 days we will collaborate together.

The goal of this Weekathon is to spend good time together, learning and improving project rather than work under pressure of time. The event is absolutely free and open for everyone willing to take a part!
